Passcode Recovery

This app is local to your browser. There is no password reset email.

Step 1: Use the Nuclear Reset button in Manage Family (if you can access it).

Step 2: Clear your browser's "Site Data/Cookies" for this URL. This wipes the app memory.

Warning: You will lose all data unless you have a recent JSON Export to re-import!
